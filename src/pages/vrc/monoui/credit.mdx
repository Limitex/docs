## Installation

Not required.

## Usage

The Credit prefab can be used to display:

- Project contributors
- Third-party assets and libraries
- Licenses and attributions
- Special thanks and acknowledgments

## Getting Started

<Steps>

### Create Credit

1. Right-click in the Hierarchy window
2. Navigate to `GameObject/Mono UI/Credit`
3. Click to create the Credit

### Place in Scene

1. The Credit will be created at the selected position in your Hierarchy
2. Adjust position and scale as needed

</Steps>
